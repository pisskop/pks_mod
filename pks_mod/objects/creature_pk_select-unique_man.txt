creature_pk_select-unique_man

*The purpose of this is to give all unique sentient humanoids their own class.  ie: elves are distinct from animal men, and gremlins are as well.

Notable non-sentients that might be confused for sentient:

- hungrey head
- fire imp
- unicorn
- gorilla/monkey
- grimeling
- molemarian
- crundle
- yet/sasquatch
