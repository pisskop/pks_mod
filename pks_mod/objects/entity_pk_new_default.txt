    - if you gave the mountain entity 2 dwarf creature tokens and 1 human, the game would spawn 2 dwarves and 1 human per instance of spawning the mountain entity.-
        - This is probably why theyre named after locations and not creatures.-
            - This also probably would mean that, in the above 2 dwarf 1 human situation, the elven 'mountain' entity would count as a mountain entity and not try to visit a dwarven fortress.-
