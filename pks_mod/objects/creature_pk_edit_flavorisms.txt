***FUR TRADE***
-While Im not interested in making a cute-animal slaughterhouse simulator, I am interested in giving more depth to the world so long as its thematic-
	-Fur trade in DF is probably extant but nowhere near as healthy as the real world, due to lower populations and the massive variety of opinions, isolated groups, isolated trading routes, etc-
		-what IS probably more healthy is superstition, religious and historical variety, and snake-oil sales, all of which can inflate or deflate the value of any one thing or another-


[SELECT_CREATURE:FOX]											-matching the value of lynx-	-AI generally gave an answer between 160-200% average cost-		-I imagine it was left at default value to prevent animal farming/slaughtering-
	[SELECT_MATERIAL:HAIR]
		[STATE_NAME:ALL_SOLID:fur]
		[STATE_ADJ:ALL_SOLID:fur]
		[MULTIPLY_VALUE:3]
	[SELECT_MATERIAL:SKIN]
		[MULTIPLY_VALUE:3]


[SELECT_CREATURE:HARP_SEAL]										-seal fur-
	[SELECT_MATERIAL:SKIN]
		[MULTIPLY_VALUE:2]


[SELECT_CREATURE:MINK]											-mink fur became popular post wwii-	-its pretty darn hard to get a quick valuation of a mink pelt, but the range seems to sit $100, which is less than older, historically traded furs when adjusted-
	[SELECT_MATERIAL:HAIR]
		[STATE_NAME:ALL_SOLID:fur]
		[STATE_ADJ:ALL_SOLID:fur]
		[MULTIPLY_VALUE:2]
